Meet Your Driver

About Keith


Coming from a humble background, I started my taxi business in Blantyre in 2016. I began by driving rented cars, and in 2023 I proudly bought my own vehicle — a milestone I worked hard for every single day.

Faith is central to my life — I am training to be a pastor, which shapes the honesty, patience, and care I bring to every journey and every passenger I meet.

I have lived my entire life in Blantyre. If you're visiting Malawi, I love sharing knowledge about the city, the best places to see, local food, cultural tips, and everything that makes Malawi the Warm Heart of Africa.

"Every ride is a chance to make someone's day better. Whether it's a quick city trip or a week-long adventure — I take that responsibility seriously."

— Keith, Your Driver
